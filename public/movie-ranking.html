My top 5 movies of all-time

Silent Hill

Deep story about a sinful town wiped off from all maps. First horror I ever watched.

Bleach

Good old anime. Interestin plot twist and cool main character.

Solo leveling

Another cool anime that has super cool visual batlle effects as well as nice main character with morals I can relate to.

Split

psycological thriller about man with multiple personalities and 3 girls that he chose as victims for his sacrifice ritual.

Incident in a Ghost land

Another horror that I will remember forever, story telling and acting sent cold shivers through my spine while I was watching.
